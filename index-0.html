Certificate Update

  • The MBA Nonmarket Strategy certificate is undergoing a revision of the Washington, Business, & the World (STRT 6555-01) course. Instead of a three-semester thread, this course is anticipated to be offered as a regular modular offering, beginning in spring 2025.
  • There are no changes to the certificate structure for students who are completing the three-semester thread course this spring (STRT 6555-02).
